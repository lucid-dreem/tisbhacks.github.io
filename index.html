About

Who Are We?

We are a group of high-schoolers from TISB consisting of coders, designers, innovators and more whose mission is to bring together like-minded people as a part of their programming journeys.

Check out our team here.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where you will use technology in order to solve real world problems. The goal is to ideate and prototype a solution that could potentially have a true impact on your community.

Why TISBHacks?

We want you to be part of the first ever iteration of TISBHacks so that you can be an instrumental part of our origin story.

We're opening this up to beginners, pros, and even non-coders, with workshops and events that guarantee a unique experience.

How to Build a Cloud-Connected AR/VR App in 15 Minutes or Less

The workshop will show how to quickly create augmented and virtual reality (AR/VR) apps with no technical skills or coding required and scale existing apps by connecting them to the cloud.

Learn how to create real-time cloud-connected AR/VR apps using the echoAR platform. Discover how AR/VR are changing the world through the demonstration of different use cases of 3D applications and live demos of AR/VR experiences.

Participants are encouraged to bring a laptop and a smartphone. Platforms for experimentation include: Google ARCore, WebXR, Vuforia, Unity-based apps, and more.

Introduction to Computer-Aided Design

This workshop will be an introduction to the basics that you need to know in order to create basic 3D models. 3D models made using Computer-Aided Design softwares are used for a variety of fields including engineering, game design, film and architecture.

Learning how to use CAD is an important skill in this digital age and it is a great base in pursuing various future courses.
